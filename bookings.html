Photo Credit: Oktoberfest

Even though Oktoberfest is more of a Bavarian thing, Berliners still take a chance to relish different types of beers and tasty pretzels. The festival is usually held in a few locations: Kurt-Schumacher-Damm, Britzer Gardens, and Alexanderplatz. It runs from the last weekend in September to the first two weeks in October.
